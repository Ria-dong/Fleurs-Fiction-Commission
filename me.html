About me

My name is Lingran Dong. I am currently majoring in Business Information Systems and I hold a Bachelor's degree in Supply Chain Management. My hobby is writing novels. I fell in love with literature when I was in primary school and have write original and fan fictions since then. In high school, I posted some articles on social media and received dozens of writing commissions. For some reasons, I have been stuck in the bottleneck for a long time, therefore, I temporary turn my goal to build a channel for writers and funders to easily find and connect with each other and ideally provide some legal protection for both sides.

Mission Statement

Fleurs Fiction Commission is a public website offering an efficient and safe channel for writers who has spare time and want to earn money through writing, and funders that looking for capable writers to make their ideas become brilliant novels, to find each other and make contraction. Unlike competing sites, which only provide these services for painters or only capable to interact through comments, Fleurs will offer a just-for-writer platform, with accurate filter to find ideal price, request and type of writer, as well as an online contract that has legal force.
